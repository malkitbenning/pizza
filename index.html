Welcome

We are a small business set up by ITPT with the help of Basit Ali. We serve the local Granton Community and employ 5 chefs all trained to the highest standards. All training has been carried out by ITPT at their Edinburgh Offices. The chefs can't even boil an egg but ask them anything about Javascript and they'll whisk you up an answer in no time!



Our Mission

To only source the freshest ingredients from local suppliers and therefore support local trades. We guarantee you won't have tasted a pizza quite like ours. We use the ITPT canteen kitchen to prepare all our pizzas so you may have to wait a while before you receive your order, but to paraphrase Jennifer Anniston ... 'we're worth it'!
